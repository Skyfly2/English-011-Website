Home of the Tech Giants
Silicon Valley and the San Francisco Bay Area has become synonomous with the tech industry. This is mainly because of the fact that it is home to countless prominent tech companies of the world as well as four of the five companies in FAANG (a popular term grouping the five most influential tech companies in the worldâ€”Facebook, Apple, Amazon, Netflix, and Google). Facebook, Apple, Netflix, and Google employ hundreds of thousands of the most highly qualified engineers in the world, and as such has caused Silicon Valley to be one of the favorite landing spots for the brightest minds looking for high-paying STEM jobs, especially right out of college. The high number of world class engineers also attracts educated and intelligent people from other disciplines as well as 57.1% of adult San Franciscans have a college degree compared to the 38% in the United States. The close proximity to these tech giants attracts people from all over the world and creates an atmosphere of productivity alongside building the local economy due to the importance of these companies to the rest of the world.
